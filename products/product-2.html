Order Management System

Streamline your business from inquiry to invoice. A comprehensive ERP platform for B2B distributors, wholesalers, and manufacturers managing high-volume orders.

Explore Capabilities Get In Touch

The Problem We Solve

Running a distribution or wholesale business means juggling dozens of moving parts: purchase orders from suppliers, stock across multiple warehouses, customer inquiries that need fast turnaround, delivery trucks on the road, challans that need to be generated and verified, and invoices that must reconcile with GST compliance.

Most businesses cobble this together with spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected software. The result? Lost orders, inventory mismatches, delayed deliveries, and revenue leakage. OMS replaces all of that with one unified platform.

Core Capabilities

Dashboard

Real-Time Business Overview

Total revenue, order counts, customer metrics, and stock levels at a glance. Sales trend charts, order status donut charts, and a recent orders table — everything your team needs to start the day with full visibility.

Sales Analytics

Seven Specialized Dashboards

Data-driven decision making with dedicated dashboards for sales, purchase, delivery, inventory, financial, customer, and inquiry analytics. Revenue trends, product performance, top customers, conversion rates — all with flexible date filtering.

Procurement

Purchase & Procurement Management

Manage your entire supply chain with a structured workflow: Purchase Advice, Purchase Orders with line items and GST calculations, Purchase Challans with weight verification, and a complete Purchase Daybook audit trail.

Inventory

Multi-Warehouse Inventory

Real-time stock visibility across all warehouse locations. Product catalogue with categories, sizes, and HSN codes. Low-stock alerts, inventory transfer workflows between warehouses, and total stock value tracking.

Logistics

Delivery & Logistics

Purpose-built for businesses managing their own fleet. Truck loading management, delivery challan generation, personnel assignment, weight reconciliation, and route tracking with vehicle and driver management.

Customers

Customer & Inquiry Management

Complete customer database with contact history, order patterns, and revenue analytics. Customer segmentation (Premium, Regular, New, Inactive), inquiry management with follow-up tracking, and a streamlined lead-to-order pipeline.

What Sets Us Apart

Built for Indian B2B

GST compliance, HSN codes, purchase daybooks, and challan generation are native — not bolted-on afterthoughts. INR formatting and weight-based pricing built in.

Operational Depth

Not a glorified spreadsheet. Handles truck loading optimization, weight reconciliation, and multi-stage purchase-to-challan pipelines.

Real-Time Visibility

Sales knows what's in stock. Delivery knows what's been ordered. Accounts know what's been invoiced. Everyone works from the same source of truth.

Excel Import/Export

Bulk import and export via .xlsx, template-based data onboarding for rapid setup, and paginated tables that handle thousands of records.

Location-Aware

Integrated OpenStreetMap support for warehouse and customer locations, enabling geographic visualization and location-based decisions.

Granular Access Control

Six built-in roles from Super Admin to Labour. Permissions enforced at navigation, page, and action level — UI adapts dynamically per user.
