Jaipur Business

A hyperlocal business networking platform where Jaipur's entrepreneurs post requirements, get trusted recommendations, and connect with verified service providers — all for free.

See How It Works Get In Touch

The Problem

Finding a reliable vendor in Jaipur is a nightmare. You ask around on WhatsApp groups, get 50 random numbers, call each one, and still don't know who to trust. Wedding photographers, interior designers, CAs, caterers — every time you need a service, you start from scratch.

For service providers, it's equally frustrating. The best vendors grow through word-of-mouth, but word-of-mouth doesn't scale. They have no way to be discovered by new clients who actually need their services right now.

Jaipur's business community needed a dedicated space — not a generic social network, not a paid directory — but a platform where real requirements meet real professionals.

How It Works

Step 1

Browse the Feed

Open the app and see what Jaipur's business community is talking about. Switch between the General Feed for all posts, Expertise Feed for industry-specific requirements, or track your own posts. Every requirement shows the category, view count, and comment count.

Step 2

Post Your Requirement

Need a wedding photographer? A CA for GST filing? An office space in C-Scheme? Create a post in seconds. Add a title, description, category, and location. Mark it urgent if you need responses fast.

Step 3

Get Trusted Recommendations

The community responds. People who've actually used a service recommend it with a verified badge. Service providers pitch directly in the comments with their experience and portfolio. No anonymous reviews, no fake listings.

Step 4

Manage All Conversations

Your Messages inbox keeps every business conversation in one place. See who responded, track ongoing negotiations, and pick up where you left off. From portfolio shares to quotations — every interaction is organized.

Step 5

Connect Directly via Chat

Found someone interesting? Message them directly on the platform. Discuss details, share portfolios, negotiate pricing — all in a private, secure chat. No need to exchange phone numbers until you're ready.

Step 6

Build Your Reputation

Every helpful comment, every successful recommendation, every positive interaction earns you community points. Your profile showcases your expertise, industry focus, and activity stats. Over time, you become the go-to person in your field.

Step 7

Shape the Community with Polls

Have a question for the business community? Run a poll. "Best coworking space in Jaipur?" "What feature should we build next?" Polls drive engagement, surface collective wisdom, and give everyone a voice.
