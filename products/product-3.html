The Problem

Instagram is where your audience lives — but engaging them manually is a full-time job. Every comment is a potential customer, every story mention an opportunity, every DM a lead. But replying to hundreds of interactions a day? That doesn't scale.

Existing tools like ManyChat charge per-contact, so your costs spiral as you grow. At 10,000 contacts, you're paying $65+/month. And their complex flow builders make simple automations feel like rocket science.

HypeZy fixes both problems — flat-rate pricing, dead-simple setup, and automations that feel genuinely human.

How It Works

Step 1

Connect Your Instagram

Securely link your Instagram Business or Creator account via Meta's official OAuth in under 30 seconds. No password sharing, no risk of account suspension. Your account stays safe because we use the same API that Meta provides to verified partners.

Step 2

Set Up Automations

Configure triggers for comments, keywords, story replies, and new followers. When someone comments "price" on your post, HypeZy instantly sends a personalized DM with your pricing.
